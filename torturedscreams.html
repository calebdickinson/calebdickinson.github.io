Tortured Screams


poem (2020)



Tortured screams of soul-dust wafting across

Razor-sharp shredders of Stygian-black wailing pumice stones

Screams of the shredded, wails of the death-nearer


Blood across tombstones, e'er drawing them closer

Blinds the sky; darkness, smoke of the foul

Wretched souls, wailing, crushed under the grindstone


Dying suns vomit across deep-creviced deserts

Screams of the water-less strangle the air
