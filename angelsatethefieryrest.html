Angels Ate the Fiery Rest


poem (2021)



Gleamish triplings whisper silence

Beyond yonder adrills tree-ills go


Trumpling creams of adoration

Tripling twauspers we must know


In confounding absolution

Angels ate the fiery rest


But in trangled words of wisdom

Wheedles thought that they knew best


In the trampling vines of twisker

Twausker audice we must go


Searching for the adrills mister

Mists of diamond fields aglow


Trampling through the flames abundant

Centaurs agle gyrings aught


While in triplings twauskers eider

Simple sanden — eiders Bäch
