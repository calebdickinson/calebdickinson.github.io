The Old Ortiber


poem (2021)



Shall we let the old ortiber blather in the wire?

Because you see the itters, and trather in the tyre

You see & heer the voices, the Trägghlw-Whagggles of Loone

But enter in the phosphors, the endless trio of doom


Thr Trigglith trasper twodder in der Auspers Venice moon

Satanic auspers triggle but the yitteers addrill soon

Shall we fear the adders of Cerena Myers/may?

But in the triggylotuh graspers do the audits trample tay?


Though lunic be these sayings but in wisdom they are brought

To search them is a pretense for the coming auspers mi

Ghourillpis for the impers yet we enter into tay

But shall we deny these sayings though they ausk of eiders hay?
