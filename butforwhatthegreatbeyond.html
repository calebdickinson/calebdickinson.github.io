But for What the Great Beyond?


poem (2021)



Ghëôring bores of audice trasper

Twinkling sighs of years gone past

Tears the flowing of the river

From our eyelids growing past


But beyond the eyelids whisper

In the adrills of divine

Trembling aders aite the auger

Timples in the distant past


Pastors of the greenish churches

Verdant in their hymns divine

Flourish noteheads of the auder

Trimpling raskers ears beshine


Yet when walkers stoop and tremble

Tearing of the rósy past

Eiders twinkle in their knowledge

Tears of knowing waidle past


Eyelids trimple of the waters

Moving fast the years go on

Yet a seeming brightness shadows

But for what the great beyond?
