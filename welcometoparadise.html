Preface


Once upon a time there were cats and mice.

The mice and cats lived together. The cats liked to eat mice. The mice did not do anything about this. They simply lived among the cats, and let the cats eat them because they had no other choice.

One day, a cat felt bad about the mice and wrote a book about how the cats were eating the mice.

Many of the mice read the book and revolted. In many places the mice overthrew the cats.

Unfortunately, as soon as the cats were removed from power, a strange thing happened. A few mice would become cats, and then there were cats again. Even worse, the mice that had become cats were even meaner than the cats that had always been cats. Many of the old cats who had always been cats were lazy and sometimes left the mice alone. The new mice that had become cats were ferocious and could not control their hunger for mice. In many areas these mice-turned-cats went on killing sprees and ate all the mice they could until the floor was splattered with blood everywhere. Now the mice had it even worse than before they got rid of the cats.

One of the first groups of mice to overthrow the cats had an ingenious solution. They got rid of the mean cats, but kept the nicest cats. They made a system where the mice and cats together would vote for only the nicest cats to have power. Although the cats would still eat the mice sometimes, the mice in this area had it nicer than any other area. These mice grew comfortable and fat.

One day a cat by the name of Dwonald Dweagan decided to try to become President of the land of the fat mice.

Instead of trying to be the nicest cat, the one that most served the interests of the mice, he had a different political idea.

He said "Think about all the regulations in this land that keep the cats from eating the mice. Don't you see how inconvenient that is for the cats?"

Because the mice were large and fat, it was easier for them to become cats here than in any other land. But the cats had many regulations that kept them from eating too many mice or getting too big and mean. Dwonald Dweagan reminded the mice of this fact.

"Think about it for a second," said Dweagan, "you mice in the land of fat mice are more likely to become cats than in any other land. Yet imagine becoming a cat, and not being able to become a big cat. What if you worked all your life to become a cat and you became a small cat instead of a big cat because of all the regulations? How is that fair to the cats?"

Many of the mice thought about it and agreed with Dwonald. They decided to make him President. Dwonald started to change the laws to make life easier for the cats and harder for the mice, but the changes were so slow most of the mice didn't notice. The mice continued to vote for cats like Dwonald to control their government even after Dwonald was no longer President.

Soon the culture changed.

Mice would write books with names like "Think like a cat and you will grow to become a cat".

There was even a giant basket that millions of mice would each throw a penny into. A random penny would be selected and the mouse who had thrown the selected penny would suddenly become a cat. The vast majority of these mice never became cats, but many of the ones that did became cats so suddenly that they did not know how to be a cat and soon became mice again.

Other mice worked hard to become cats, and although most of them never became cats, some of them became so big they were not mice anymore. They were rats. The rats were bigger than the mice, but not big enough to be cats. The rats were not mice or cats, so they were called "The Middle Class" because the were in between the mice and cats.

A lot of the mice liked to think of themselves as rats even though they weren't. Some of the rats thought they were cats so they voted for the interests of the cats and some of the rats thought they were mice so they voted for the interests of the mice. Some of the mice thought the rats were cats even though they were basically just big mice. Many of the cats felt sorry for the mice so they voted in the interests of the mice and many of the mice thought of themselves as temporarily embarrassed cats so they voted in the interest of the cats.

As time went on the two political parties served the interests of the cats over the mice more and more. Soon there was a political party that claimed to serve the interests of the cats and served the interests of the cats. There was also a political party that claimed to serve the interest of the mice but also served the interest of the cats.

Unfortunately, the mice were so dumb and fat at this point that many of them didn't even vote for the party that claimed to serve the interest of the mice. Many of them voted for the party that claimed to serve the interest of the cats.

Slowly, the cats in the land of the fat mice grew larger and more powerful as the cats slowly gained power over the mice.
