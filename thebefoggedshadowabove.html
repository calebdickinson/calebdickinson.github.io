The Befogged Shadow Above


flash fiction (2020)



A translucent, sylphlike luminescence gleams through the darkful night, O Daughter of Elysium. Methought I apprehend an exiguous consonance emanating from a distant Aëolian harp?

Throughout and amidst the terrible expanse of waste and time that casts the land in shadow; strange, dark creatures; the tread of basilisks o'erheaps the firmament with trembling.

The sun and the moon have concealed their visage. The befogged Shadow above, an inky Stygian hue, weaves and braids in a knot of turbulent maelstroms, drowning the sky.

Yet half a dozen rods from where I lie, prone and bruised upon the sand, sighing and gasping upon the toxic effluvium of this accursed archipelago, I perceive, thru' the clammy mist, even a manifest ephemeral, fugacious coup d'œil of th' starboard boom of a caraval.

Yet at that moment, as I begin to suffer a minute flicker of hope, the soul-crushing clang of a keeling boat run aground by the breakers assaults my ears, even as the tempest presently intensifies. Lightning strikes the ship, and the anguished screams of dying sailors fill my heart with consternation and boundless horror.

Throughout the gleam of the trembling shadows I sense a consciousness proximate. Heretofore I had supposed myself to be the exclusive human inhabitant animate here, in this accursed Isle, known in the tongue of Arabia as Jazirat Alrajim, but to the native Lovecraftian terrors; QUARANTINE.
